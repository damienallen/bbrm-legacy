The Black Biomedical Research Movement is a not for profit organization based in Westchesters, NY. BBRM is a UCB College of Arts and Sciences sponsored-research program. The movement was established in 1997 to aggressively promote health consciousness and involvement in all aspects of biomedical research to African Americans.
