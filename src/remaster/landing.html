“A Relentless Effort to be as Concious of Our Health as We are of Our History”

Started in 1997, the Black Biomedical Research Movement has promoted and advocated for health awareness and literacy in the Black community.

This website, available online since the year 2000, has served as a vital organizational tool for outreach and communication.

As a means of perserving the history and achivements of the Movement, this site has been remastered for accessible viewing on modern devices.
